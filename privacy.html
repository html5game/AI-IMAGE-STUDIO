This Privacy Policy describes how AI Image Studio ("we," "us," or "our") handles your information when you use our web application.

Information We Process

When you use our service, you may upload images and provide text prompts. This data is sent directly to the Google Gemini API for processing to fulfill your request (e.g., to generate an image, edit an image, or analyze an image). We do not store your uploaded images or prompts on our servers after the request is completed.

Data Storage

The application uses your browser's `localStorage` to save your generation/edit history for your convenience. This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. You can clear this history at any time within the application.

Third-Party Services

Our service relies on the Google Gemini API. Your interactions are subject to Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. We are not responsible for the data practices of Google.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
