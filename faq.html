Frequently Asked Questions

What AI model powers this application?

AI Image Studio is powered by Google's Gemini family of models, including specialized models for image generation (Imagen) and vision understanding.

Is there a cost to use this service?

This application is a demonstration of the capabilities of the Gemini API. Usage is subject to the terms and pricing of the Google AI Platform. Please refer to Google's official documentation for details.

What happens to the images I upload?

Your privacy is important to us. Uploaded images are sent to the Google Gemini API for processing and are not stored on our servers. Please review our Privacy Policy for more details on data handling.

Why did my image generation fail?

Image generation or editing can sometimes fail due to safety filters that prevent the creation of harmful or inappropriate content. It could also be due to an overly complex or ambiguous prompt. Try rephrasing your prompt to be more specific and clear.
